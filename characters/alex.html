Alexander Kenning

About

Alex is a private detective with the ability to interact with the hidden world of the supernatural. He works for the witch Sylvia, tracking down rare magical artifacts for her shop in exchange for access to the world of magic, which is critical to his search for his missing sister.

Personality

Intelligent, Observant, Sarcastic
His sarcastic sense of humor and keen observation make him an ideal character to point out and comment on the absurdity of the world around him. He fancies himself a cool, debonaire ladie's man, but somehow always manages to embarrass himself. While he tries to act cool and disinterested most of the time, he actually has a good heart and will eventually do the right thing, even if it is begrudgingly.

Major Story Beats

Backstory

Alex is a private detective trying to solve a case involving the abduction of his sister. He catches the eye of the witch Sylvia after he is able to follow the kidnapper's trail, despite the involvement of supernatural elements. Sylvia strikes a deal with Alex that she will provide him access to the world of magic, aiding his search, as long as he agrees to work for her. The job involves finding and retrieving rare magical artifacts for Sylvia's shop.
