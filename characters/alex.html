Alexander Kenning

Origin Story

Alex, considered a child prodigy, became a world famous detective at age 15. He rose to fame by helping Interpol solve several tough international cases. He fell from grace at age 20 while investigating the disappearance of his sister (along with several other women), after becoming obsessed and continuing to insist that their disappearance was supernatural in origin.

Personality

Intelligent, Observant, Sarcastic
