Alexandria Marconia (Marc) Hazelthorne

About

Alexandria is a recent graduate from the North American Witch Academy. She is doing her customary post graduate witch internship with the renowned witch Sylvia. Because there was already an Alex working at the shop, the gang calls her Marc (a shortened version of her middle name) at Sylvia's instruction. Alexandria has a strong sense of justice, and wants to use her powers for good. However, these values start slowly eroding the more time she spends around Sylvia and her circle.

Personality

Intelligent, Just, Optimistic, Rule Follower, Energetic
