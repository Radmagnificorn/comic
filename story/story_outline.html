Story

Log Line

A detective and a recent Magic Academy graduate repossess cursed items when the owners are unable to pay the price.

Premise Summary

Sylvia is a witch who owns a shop that is renowned throughout the supernatural world. "The Shop", as it's simply called, collects and sells some of the most powerful magical artifacts in existence. These artifacts don't come without a steep price however. Alex is a detective who was hired after he was able to track down a supernatural serial killer without the aid of magic.
